Welcome, my name is John Frazier and i am a Frontend wed developer. I am from and live in New York City. My road to web development was a pretty different one. I went to college to become a mechanical engineer which i quickly realized i had no passion for. So i got a job and spent 10 year working in retail. Over time i got to the point where i just knew it wasn’t for me anymore.

My road to web development really started when a friend on mine who is a web developer proposed to question “why don’t you try this?”. He showed me a few things but at the time i did have much in the way of free time. It took me whole year before i took my real first step by enrolling at New York Code and Design Academy and took two nightly courses(Web Development and Frontend basics). After that i took me another year before i took a leap of faith and enrolled in their software engineering intensive course.

Now I’m looking to for an environment where i can learn and grow my skills.
