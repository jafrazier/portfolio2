Welcome

Welcome, my name is John Frazier and I am a Frontend Web Developer. I am from and live in New York City. My road to web development was a pretty different one. I went to college to become a Mechanical Engineer, which I quickly realized I had no passion for. I spent 10 year working in retail. Over time I got to a point where I just knew it wasn’t for me anymore.

My road to web development really started when a friend on mine who is a web developer proposed a question “why don’t you try this?”. He showed me a few things but at the time I didn't have much free time. It took me a whole year before I took my first real step by enrolling at New York Code and Design Academy and took two nightly courses(Web Development and Frontend basics). After that it took me another year before I took a leap of faith and enrolled in their software engineering intensive course.

Now I’m looking for an environment where I can learn and grow my skills.
